About us

Welcome to the official website of the UNSW Redback Racing Formula SAE Team. We are a student society that designs, manufactures and tests an open-wheel Formula SAE specification racecar. As a Sydney-based team, we compete in the annual FSAE-Australiasia Competition held each December, which pitches our car against those made by other universities in the Asia-Pacific region.

Our team

We are a diverse team made up of UNSW students studying anything from engineering and computer science to business.
